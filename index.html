Thanks again for coming in to test with us!

We've put together some important information about what will happen when you come in to the lab.

How to get here

Your session will be held at the City University Interaction Lab:

College Building City, University of London 280 St John Street London EC1V 4PB

Google map

If you have trouble finding the lab on the day, please call 07xxxxxxxxx and ask for Alex

What will happen

On the day:

  • - You'll arrive at the College Building and let reception know you're here.
  • - One of our research team will come down and meet you, and take you through some forms and information about the day.
  • - You'll be taken through to the lab to meet the researcher running your session. (Don't worry, they don't bite!)
  • - The researcher will show you a website and ask you to carry out some tasks. It'll be just like when you take the tests at home, but more of a conversation instead of reading instructions from the screen.

That's it! Once your session is over, you'll be given some information to take away, and your payment will be processed as usual.

If you can't make it

Plans change, and unexpected things come up, so you might need to let us know you can't make it.

It's not a problem if this happens, but please let us know as soon as possible so that we can find someone to come in instead.

If you know more than 24 hours beforehand, then please email users@whatusersdo.com.

If something happens on the day, then please call Alex on 07xxxxxxxxx.

Getting in touch

If you have any questions about the day, please get in touch with the panel team at users@whatusersdo.com
