On the day

Here's what you can expect to happen on the day:

  • location_on You'll arrive at the College Building and let reception know you're here to meet with Alexander Darlington.
  • mode_edit One of our research team will come down and meet you, and take you through some forms and information about the day.
  • people You'll be taken through to the lab to meet the researcher running your session. (Don't worry, they don't bite!)
  • laptop_mac The researcher will show you a website and ask you to carry out some tasks. It'll be just like when you take the tests at home, but more of a conversation instead of reading instructions from the screen.
  • doneThat's it! Once your session is over, you'll be given some information to take away, and your payment will be processed as usual.

If you can't make it

Plans change, and unexpected things come up, so you might need to let us know you can't make it.

It's not a problem if this happens, but please let us know as soon as possible so that we can find someone to come in instead.

If you know more than 24 hours beforehand, then please email users@whatusersdo.com.

If something happens on the day, then please call Alex on 07549 224230.
